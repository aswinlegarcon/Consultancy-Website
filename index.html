Success Stories

Pillar India Partnership

Lead Consultancy & Training collaborated with Pillar India to support an advertising shoot aimed at promoting their Career Guidance Program. This strategic partnership strengthened brand visibility, inspired aspiring learners, and maximized outreach by showcasing impactful guidance opportunities for future academic and professional success.

Innovative Case Study Approaches

Lead Consultancy & Training, under KPR Global Edutech Consulting Services, conducted a one-day Management Development Program on "Innovative Case Study Approaches," attended by academicians and industrialists. Nearly 30 participants gained practical insights to strengthen pedagogical skills and enhance case-based teaching effectiveness.

MoU with EazyLink Academy

KPRCAS, through its Centre for International Affairs and Lead Consultancy & Training, signed an MoU with EazyLink Academy to strengthen academic collaboration and global learning opportunities. The partnership aims to enhance training, skill development, and international exposure for students and faculty.

Global Education

The Centre for International Affairs and Lead Consultancy & Training jointly organized a career guidance program on "Insights of Global Education and Career Opportunities." Experts from Griffith College, Ireland, and EazyLink Academy addressed students, offering valuable perspectives on international education pathways and emerging global career prospects.

Web Design and Development

Lead Consultancy & Training developed a complete website for the campus startup Fruitacy, enhancing its digital presence and brand identity. The platform was designed to showcase products, improve customer reach, and support the startup's growth with a clean, user-friendly interface.

Pan Card Mela

Lead Consultancy & Training organized a PAN Card Mela for students from various Educational Institutions, providing on-campus assistance for PAN card registration and verification. Nearly 200 students participated and benefited from this service, making the initiative highly impactful and student-centric.
